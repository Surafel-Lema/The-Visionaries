Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities

Reduce inequality within and among countries. Goal 10 aims to empower all individuals and groups to participate in decision-making processes that affect their lives, ensuring equal opportunities for all.

Did You Know?

Income inequality has increased significantly in many countries over the past few decades. Goal 10 emphasizes the need for equitable distribution of resources and opportunities.

Targets and Indicators

Target 10.1: Achieve Income Growth

Achieve and sustain income growth of the bottom 40% of the population at a rate higher than the national average.

Indicator: Growth rate of household expenditure or income per capita among the bottom 40% of the population.

Target 10.2: Empower and Promote Social, Economic, and Political Inclusion

Empower and promote the social, economic, and political inclusion of all, irrespective of age, gender, disability, race, ethnicity, origin, religion, or economic or other status.

Indicator: Proportion of people living in households with access to basic services.

How You Can Take Action

Advocate for Equality

Raise your voice for equality by participating in local initiatives and advocating for policies that promote inclusivity and equal opportunities.

Volunteer for Organizations

Get involved with organizations that focus on reducing inequalities and helping marginalized communities.

Educate Yourself and Others

Learn about the challenges faced by different groups and educate others to foster a culture of understanding and acceptance.
