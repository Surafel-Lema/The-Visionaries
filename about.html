About Sustainable Development Goals

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a collection of 17 global goals set by the United Nations in 2015 to achieve a better and more sustainable future for everyone by 2030. These goals address global challenges, including poverty, inequality, climate change, environmental degradation, peace, and justice.
