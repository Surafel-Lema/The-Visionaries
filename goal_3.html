Goal 3: Good Health and Well-Being

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. Goal 3 of the Sustainable Development Goals seeks to reduce mortality rates, address communicable diseases, and promote mental health and well-being globally.

Did You Know?

Every year, around 5 million children under the age of 5 die from preventable diseases. Achieving Goal 3 requires comprehensive strategies and global cooperation to ensure access to quality healthcare for everyone.

Targets and Indicators

Target 3.1: Reduce Maternal Mortality

Reduce the global maternal mortality ratio to less than 70 per 100,000 live births by 2030.

Indicator: Maternal mortality ratio.

Target 3.2: End Epidemics

End the epidemics of AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria, and neglected tropical diseases by 2030.

Indicator: Incidence and prevalence of these diseases.

How You Can Take Action

Promote Healthy Lifestyles

Encourage your peers and family to adopt healthy lifestyles through proper nutrition, regular exercise, and mental wellness practices.

Volunteer in Health Programs

Get involved with local health organizations and initiatives that promote health education and disease prevention.

Raise Awareness

Share information about health issues and encourage discussions around mental health, nutrition, and public health policies.
