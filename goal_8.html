Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

Promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all. Goal 8 aims to achieve higher levels of economic productivity while ensuring equal opportunities for all individuals.

Did You Know?

Globally, around 1.4 billion people are unemployed or working in conditions that do not provide decent wages. Achieving Goal 8 will ensure that everyone has access to fair work and economic opportunities.

Targets and Indicators

Target 8.1: Sustained Economic Growth

Promote sustained economic growth of at least 7% per year in developing countries.

Indicator: Annual growth rate of real GDP per capita.

Target 8.5: Full Employment

Achieve full and productive employment and decent work for all women and men.

Indicator: Unemployment rate, by sex, age, and persons with disabilities.

How You Can Take Action

Support Local Businesses

Buy from and support local businesses to help strengthen your community's economy and create jobs.

Advocate for Fair Wages

Promote fair wages and working conditions in your school and community. Encourage businesses to pay fair wages.

Participate in Career Development Programs

Engage in career development initiatives that enhance skills and create better employment opportunities for yourself and others.
