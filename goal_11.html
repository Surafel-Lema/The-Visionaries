Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. Goal 11 aims to ensure access to safe and affordable housing, and to upgrade slum settlements.

Targets and Indicators

Target 11.1: Ensure Access to Safe and Affordable Housing

Ensure access for all to adequate, safe, and affordable housing and basic services and upgrade slum settlements.

Indicator: Proportion of urban population living in slums, informal settlements, or inadequate housing.

Target 11.2: Provide Access to Safe, Affordable, and Sustainable Transport Systems

Provide access to safe, affordable, accessible, and sustainable transport systems for all, with a focus on vulnerable populations.

Indicator: Proportion of population that has convenient access to public transport.
