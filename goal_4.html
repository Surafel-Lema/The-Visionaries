Goal 4: Quality Education

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all. Goal 4 focuses on ensuring that every child has access to quality education, regardless of their background.

Did You Know?

Over 260 million children and youth are out of school globally. Achieving Goal 4 means making education accessible to every child, leading to greater equality and opportunities.

Targets and Indicators

Target 4.1: Quality Education

Ensure that all girls and boys complete free, equitable and quality primary and secondary education leading to relevant and effective learning outcomes.

Indicator: Proportion of children and young people achieving at least a minimum proficiency level in reading and mathematics.

Target 4.5: Equal Access

Eliminate gender disparities in education and ensure equal access to all levels of education and vocational training for the vulnerable, including persons with disabilities, indigenous peoples, and children in vulnerable situations.

Indicator: Parity indices of gender in education.

How You Can Take Action

Support Local Schools

Volunteer your time or resources to support local schools, especially in underserved areas. Consider tutoring or mentoring students.

Promote Education Equity

Raise awareness about the importance of equal access to education for all and support initiatives that work towards educational equity.

Advocate for Education Policies

Get involved in advocacy efforts aimed at improving educational policies and funding to ensure all children receive quality education.
