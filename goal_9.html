Goal 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation. Goal 9 emphasizes the importance of technology and infrastructure in achieving sustainable development.

Did You Know?

More than 2 billion people lack access to reliable internet services. Achieving Goal 9 is essential for promoting economic growth and sustainable development.

Targets and Indicators

Target 9.1: Develop Quality, Reliable Infrastructure

Develop quality, reliable, sustainable, and resilient infrastructure to support economic development and human well-being.

Indicator: Proportion of the rural population who live within 2 km of all-season roads.

Target 9.5: Enhance Research and Upgrade Technological Capabilities

Enhance scientific research, upgrade the technological capabilities of industrial sectors in all countries.

Indicator: Research and development expenditure as a proportion of GDP.

How You Can Take Action

Support Innovation

Encourage local innovation by participating in community programs that promote technology and creative solutions.

Get Involved in Infrastructure Projects

Join efforts in your community to improve local infrastructure, such as roads, public transport, and internet access.

Advocate for Sustainable Practices

Promote sustainable practices in local industries and support businesses that prioritize innovation and sustainability.
